## What is Probo?

Probo is an open-source compliance management platform that helps organizations achieve and maintain compliance across multiple frameworks — including SOC 2, ISO 27001, ISO 27701, ISO 42001, GDPR, HIPAA, CCPA, FERPA, CASA, NIS2, and DORA. Built for startups and growing companies, Probo provides risk management, evidence collection, vendor management, policy management, and audit preparation tools.
