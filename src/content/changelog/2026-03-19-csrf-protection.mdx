We've added cross-origin request forgery (CSRF) protection across the platform. This prevents malicious websites from making unauthorized requests on your behalf when you're logged into Probo.

If you're working with multiple browser tabs open — which, let's be honest, is always — this ensures that actions in Probo can only come from Probo. One more layer of defense for your compliance data.
