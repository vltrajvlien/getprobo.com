Every action in your organization is now logged and visible in one place. Who signed in, who updated a policy, who invited a new team member, when it happened, and from where.

This is the kind of observability auditors love to see. During a SOC 2 or ISO 27001 review, one of the first questions is "can you show me an audit trail?" Now you can, without exporting anything or stitching together data from different sources.

The logs are SIEM-ready, so if your security team already uses a centralized logging platform, they can pull Probo events right into their existing dashboards and alerting workflows.
