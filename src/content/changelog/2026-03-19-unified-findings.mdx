Audit findings are now a first-class concept across all of Probo. Whether you're working in the web app, using the MCP integration with your AI assistant, or managing things from the CLI — findings work everywhere, the same way.

Create, update, and track findings from whichever interface fits your workflow. Link them to audits, assign severity levels, and follow resolution progress without switching tools.

We've also introduced clearer severity levels: findings are now categorized as **minor nonconformity**, **major nonconformity**, or **observation** — giving you and your auditors a shared language for prioritization.
